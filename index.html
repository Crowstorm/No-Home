Currently working: 2nd and 3rd level. Rebuilding 1st level to serve as tutorial

Game is optimised to be played on desktops and laptops. It should work on most mobile devices, however, turning your screen to the side is recommended

In order to travel to next locations you will need to click objects like doors or entrances.

Sometimes characters will talk to you automatically. Other times it will be up to you to interact with them

If you see an interesting item you can try to pick it up by clicking on it. Who knows, it may come in handy

To use an item, click on it, then click on desired object.

Sometimes you may be asked to answer some questions or type in passwords to proceed. Type your answers into forms

Dont give up when you're frustrated, answer may be simpler than you think. Sometimes you may find additional tips if you try long enough.

If you find a minor bug or one stopping you from finishing the game contact me at mateuszmazurofficial@gmail.com or via medium where you found the game
